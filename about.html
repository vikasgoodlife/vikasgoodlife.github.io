About Lumia

Importer and wholesale distributor of UPVC profiles used for windows and doors in housing and commercial projects.

Our Mission

To support fabricators and construction professionals with innovative products and exceptional service — ensuring timely supply, technical support, and consistent quality so projects run smoothly.

What We Offer

  • Wide range of UPVC profiles: casement, sliding, tilt & turn, french doors, fixed frames and more.
  • Bulk supply & logistics: reliable importing and distribution to support project timelines.
  • Fabricator support: technical drawings, cutting-to-spec, and on-demand reorders.
  • Quality assurance: pre-shipment inspection and consistent QC processes.

Values

Integrity

Transparent pricing, honest timelines, dependable service.

Partnership

Long-term partnerships with fabricators and contractors.

Innovation

Continuously sourcing improved profiles and hardware options.
